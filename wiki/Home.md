# NenasKita Wiki

Welcome to the NenasKita documentation.

**NenasKita** is a mobile application developed to digitalize the pineapple farming industry in Melaka, Malaysia. Built in collaboration with **LPNM (Lembaga Perindustrian Nanas Malaysia)**.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Flutter 3.32.8, Dart 3.8.1
- **State**: Riverpod, Freezed
- **Backend**: Firebase (Firestore, Auth, FCM)
- **Maps**: OpenStreetMap (flutter_map)
- **Storage**: Cloudinary CDN
